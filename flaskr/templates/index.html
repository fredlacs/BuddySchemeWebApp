FAQ

Is it free?

It is Free! This is thanks to our helping mentors!

How do I become a mentee?

Once you sign up as a mentee, you can specify your preferences, based on which our state-of-the-art algorithm will assign you a mentor.

How do I become a mentor?

Once you sign up as a mentor, you can specify your preferences. We will take care of assigning you however mentees you can handle based on what interests you have in common.

Do you use my data?

We only use your data to match you to the best mentor/mentee and we do not distribute your data to any third parties. You can also request that all your data to be delete, in compliance with the GDPR.
